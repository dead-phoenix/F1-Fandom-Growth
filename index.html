When your driver wins, your country watches
Mexico's interest exploded +211% the year Pérez joined Red Bull. The Netherlands fell even as Verstappen dominated. The relationship between driver success and national interest is real — but complicated. Hover dots for details.

Netflix changed everything — or did it?
The US grew +134.8% with no competitive driver and no home race until 2022. That growth has to be media-driven. But the peak month heatmap reveals something subtler — race calendar timing still dominates for host nations. Drive to Survive shifted when people search, not just how many.
